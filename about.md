# Platformer Save!

Hi! I know you've encountered a situation where you're going through a very long platformer and then suddenly your game crashes, you accidentally quit, or your phone dies! 

All your hard work and progress are gone in a second. Pretty annoying, right?

**Platformer Save** is here to fix that! This mod automatically backs up your position every single time you hit a checkpoint. If you leave the level or close the game, you will instantly teleport back to your last saved checkpoint next time you enter. 

### Key Features:
* **Instant Auto-Save** — Saves your exact position the moment you touch a checkpoint.
* **Smart Teleport** — Automatically spawns you at your save point when you enter the level.
* **No Lag & No Setup** — Works completely in the background without any annoying pop-up menus.

No progress lost, no time wasted!
